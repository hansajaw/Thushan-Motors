Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Thushan Motors respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, place an order, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, order details, and payment method information.

Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we may receive basic profile information such as your name and email address. We use this only to create or log in to your Thushan Motors account.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to manage your account, process orders, arrange delivery, respond to inquiries, improve our website, and provide customer support.

Payment Information

Our website may show card payment options for checkout. We do not store sensitive card information such as CVV. For live online payments, payment processing should be handled by a secure payment gateway.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share necessary delivery details with delivery partners only to complete your order.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

Your Rights

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information where applicable.
